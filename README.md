### 🚀 About Me

I'm Richard, a mechatronics engineer and CTO/Head of R&amp;D at Vhorde Technologies, a Nigerian deep-tech hardware startup I co-founded. I lead technical development across multiple product lines, building AI-powered assistive technology that solves real-world accessibility challenges for people living with disabilities. Currently studying Mechatronics, Robotics, and Automation Engineering at the University of Benin. When I'm not building for Vhorde, I'm designing everything from exoskeletons to electric vehicle conversions in my own workshop.

🔭 &nbsp;I'm currently working on **IRIS and ARES, two AI-powered assistive tech products at Vhorde Technologies**  
🌱 &nbsp;I'm currently learning **Mechatronics, Robotics, and Automation Engineering at the University of Benin**  
👯 &nbsp;I'm looking to collaborate on **hardware engineering and embedded systems projects, especially locally manufactured tech solutions in Nigeria**  
💬 &nbsp;Ask me about **assistive technology, embedded systems, or my exoskeleton suit and E30 electric conversion side projects**  
😄 &nbsp;Pronouns: **he/him**  
⚡ &nbsp;Fun fact: **I'm converting a 1991 BMW 325i E30 into an EV in my spare time, and separately building a patent-pending exoskeleton suit inspired by G.I. Joe's Delta-6 Accelerator Suits**
